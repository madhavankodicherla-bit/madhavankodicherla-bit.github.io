LifeHub Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you sign in to LifeHub using Google OAuth, we collect your name, email address, and profile photo from your Google account. We also store data you create in the app including tasks, calendar events, travel plans, and financial records.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide the LifeHub personal productivity service. We do not sell, share, or distribute your personal data to third parties.

3. Google Services

LifeHub uses Google OAuth for authentication and optionally connects to Google Calendar. Our use of Google services complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers using industry-standard encryption. Financial data and sensitive information is encrypted at rest.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as you have an active account. You can request deletion of all your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights contact us at madhavankodicherla@gmail.com.

7. Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

8. Children's Privacy

LifeHub is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by posting the new policy on this page.

10. Contact Us
